Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to build revenue for our business by providing information in an easily accessible way online to rafters just beginning!

Audience

We are looking for those just starting out on this amazing new sport! We are looking for individuals with children over the age of 8 who feel too busy to get outside, but would like to. We will help them not only find the time, and afford the adventure, but also have a worry-free experience! These individuals will typically accessible the site on a mobile device through social media links.
